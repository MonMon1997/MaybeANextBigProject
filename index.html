    Experience Fine Dining with a Beautiful View

    Situated along the scenic river at the New West Quay, The Boathouse is the premier dining destination for a distinctively west coast menu of fresh seafood and premium steak. Grab seating in the stylish dining room or bar for freshly shucked oysters and stunning waterfront views; or out on the seasonal open-air patio for a closer look at the riverboats. The Boathouse at New Westminster is open for breakfast, brunch, lunch, and dinner daily.

    *To make a reservation for parties of 8 or more people, please call the restaurant in advance.
